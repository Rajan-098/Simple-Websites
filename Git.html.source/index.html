Global Institute of Engineering and Technology (GIET), established in 2009, is a private institute affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the discipline of Engineering. Admission in the GIET is done on the basis of the TNEA entrance exam.
The institute has a dedicated Training and Placement Cell who provides 100% placement assistance to its students. More than 91% of students get placed through the campus placement every year.
